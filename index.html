Write your Will in 5 easy steps

Create & Verify Account
  • Register on WillPower by entering the requested information.
  • Open the verification link sent to your email.
  • Use the OTP (One-Time Password) sent to your registered mobile number and email address to complete registration.
  • After registration, log on to your WillPower account.
Choose Document from the Templates Provided
  • Choose your document template from the available options.
Pay for your Chosen Document
  • Make payment for the chosen template.
Fill, Print and Sign your Document
  • Enter required information in the fields provided.
  • Click Finalize when done. (Note: You have 30 days from date of payment to finalize document. After that you will need to make payment again.)
  • Check your email for the encrypted document and DHL waybill sent from WillPower.
  • Unlock document with Document Password sent to your registered mobile number and registered email address.
  • Print document and DHL waybill.
Seal and Take to Nearest DHL office for Dispatch
  • Seal document for dispatch.
  • Take sealed document and DHL waybill to the nearest DHL office for dispatch to UTL Trust Management Services.

**including administrative fees.
