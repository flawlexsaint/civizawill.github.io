The RSA Will is a will that one should use to distribute Liquid assets only. i.e. Retirement Savings Account, Cash Accounts, Gratuities, Life Insurances and other employment related entitlements. Simple Will is a will used for distribution of ALL ASSETS i.e. It comprises the liquid assets and physical assets like Land, Houses, intellectual Properties, jewelry etc.

When you start out in life, you get a good job that comes with benefits, a Retirement Savings Account, gratuity entitlements and Group life insurance… All these; usually in error, are not considered assets on behalf of the testator leading to individuals not creating wills when they should. After demise most of these are forfeited and lost. We have thus created this to capture the liquid assets of individuals to ensure that they will be accessible to beneficiaries /family members unfettered. In other words, you don’t have to wait until you have lands, houses, cars, businesses before you create a will.

RSA Will is an estate planning vehicle for distribution of Liquid assets only. Liquid assets include; RSA (Retirement Savings Accounts), Employee benefits like pension plans, group life insurance, life insurance, cash accounts etc. They do not include real and personal property like land, cars, houses, jewelry etc.

Simple Will is an estate planning vehicle for distribution of all assets. Assets include; Real and personal property like land, cars, houses, jewelry etc. and Liquid assets like RSA (Retirement Savings Accounts), Employee benefits like pension plans, group life insurance, life insurance, cash accounts etc.
