Privacy Policy

UTL Trust Management Services Limited (UTL), a company incorporated in Nigeria [RC 4834] with its business offices at ED Building, 47 Marina, 2nd Floor Lagos State, Nigeria is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.

For the purposes of data protection laws in Nigeria, the data controller is UTL. Your privacy is important to us. To properly protect your privacy, we are providing this notice to explain our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. To ensure that this notice is easy to find, we have made it available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.

The Information We Collect

This notice applies to all information collected or submitted on the WillPower.com website. Within this site you can complete your Will and Trust online. The types of personal information collected during this process are: Names, addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, etc.

The Way We Use Information

Our goal is to be your trusted source for Estate Planning information and Will preparation. When you complete your Will online, you are required to provide private and personal information. This may include your name, address, email, and information necessary to complete your document. We use the information you provide about yourself when completing your documents. We use return email addresses to answer the email we receive and to deliver Will documents. We use non-identifying and aggregate information to improve the design of our website. We may contact you by email or phone. We may send promotional emails and newsletters from time to time to users who have registered on the site and to those who have opted in to receive such emails.

As we develop our business, we may buy or sell assets or business offerings. Customer, member, transaction, email, and visitor information is generally one of the business assets in these types of transactions. We may also transfer such information in the course of corporate divestitures, mergers, or dissolution.

The nature of our services require that we share personal information with our insurance company. Information which we may share include

  • Name
  • Name of Beneficiary(ies)
  • Address
  • Telephone Number
  • Utility Bill
  • Passport Photo
  • Valid means of ID
  • Any other information which will assist the insurance company in the performance of its duty.

We use industry standard encryption algorithms for securing our customers personal information in a highly encrypted format. The design of WillPower ensures that we never use or share the personally identifiable information provided to us online in ways unrelated to the one described above without also providing you an opportunity to opt-out or otherwise prohibit such unrelated uses.

Where we store personal data

All information you provide to us is stored on secure servers. Where we have given you (or where you have chosen) a password which enables you to access certain parts of our website, you are responsible for keeping this password confidential. You must not share your password with anyone.

Period of storage

Where you register on our website and enter personal data but do not complete the process and create your Will, we will retain your data for a period of time necessary to ensure that we are able to assist you should you have any questions or feedback in relation to our services.

Our Commitment to Data Security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Changes to our privacy policy

Any changes we make to our privacy policy in the future will be posted on this page. Please check back frequently to see any updates or changes to our privacy policy.

How to Contact Us

Should you have other questions or concerns about these privacy policies, please send us an email at support@willpower.ng
