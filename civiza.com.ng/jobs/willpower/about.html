About WillPower

Over five decades of consistent professional expertise and experience in diverse kinds of trust transactions.

Putting assets into a Will or a Trust is now easy and hassle free.

WillPower is an online estate planning service that simplifies the process of creating a Will or a Trust. With WillPower, Nigerians, regardless of their ethnicity or religion, can create a Will or Trust from the comfort of their homes, offices or on the go letting them determine how assets will be transferred and distributed among loves ones.

Willpower is an initiative to help secure and empower the future of the breadwinners’ assets and beneficiaries. Now getting one's affairs in order, is affordable, convenient, and easy.

WillPower is a service offering of UTL Trust Management Services Limited. UTL Trustees has paved the way to making estate planning and financial planning services affordable, simple and available to more people than ever before.

Many people put off end-of-life planning, either because it’s uncomfortable to think about, or because they don't know where to start. Not having a plan means not protecting your loved ones, which is why WillPower has set out to make it easy to get started from the comfort of your home.

WillPower is a service that millions of Nigerians can take advantage of and rely upon. The peace of mind that comes with planning your estate on WillPower allows people to then focus on what is really important—taking care of their families and building strong businesses.

Keeping faith because you are in good company.

UTL Trust Management Services Limited (formerly Union Trustees Limited) has over five decades of consistent professional expertise and experience in diverse kinds of Trust transactions.

Today, UTL is engaged in the business of Public, Corporate and Private Trusteeship as well as Fund/Portfolio Management with excellent record of service delivery. We are duly licensed by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) as a Trustee and Funds/Portfolio Manager.
